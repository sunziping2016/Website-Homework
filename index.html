Welcome

Who are we?

Fansinated by modern Artificial Intelligence technology, we gathered together to build a website to inform the ordinary people of the coming of a new era, a era belonging to these intelligence machine crafted by us. They will, surely, benefit us a lot. This is who we are, the people trying to bring the new era near.

What's our goal?

It has been long that AI is getting known by ordinary people mainly via public media. They are, to some extents, not professional, not interactive and not infomative. We are donated to spreading the knowledge related to AI.

Why AlphaGo?

It was really a hot affair when this site was initially created(Mar 10th, 2016). At that time, a five-game Go match was going on between South Korean professional Go player i>Lee Sedol and a computer Go program developed by Google DeepMind, AlphaGo. And thus, AlphaGo has become some kind of symbol of top AI as well as the theme of this website.
